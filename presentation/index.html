Introducing OperatorOS

Your heads-up before
the first time at the machine

First time at a manufacturing machine? Even a basic tutorial can feel overwhelming.

OperatorOS helps beginners understand what they're looking at — before they touch the controls. We're not replacing trained instructors. We bridge the gap until your first hands-on session.
